ABOUT US

Eco & Lyfe

EcoLyfe is a distinguished forest conservation consultant firm with a profound dedication to preserving and restoring the world's forests. With a wealth of expertise in sustainable forestry practices and ecological conservation.

Vision & Mission

Our Vision:

Our vision is of forests standing tall and vibrant, teeming with diverse flora and fauna, providing essential habitats, clean air, and water resources.

Our Mision:

Our mission is to be a trusted partner and catalyst for positive change in forest conservation efforts globally.

Why CCB Aspect

Climate, Community, & Biodiversity (CCB)/Verified Carbon Standards (VCS)

We specialize in guiding organizations through the complex process of developing and verifying carbon credit projects. Our expertise in project design, methodology selection, compliance, and verification, helping clients to create high-quality carbon offset projects that meet rigorous international standards. By leveraging our knowledge of carbon markets and sustainability, we enable businesses to contribute to climate change mitigation while potentially generating additional revenue streams.
